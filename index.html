About Me

My name is Razar (ɹˈɑːzə) , and I'm a newly certified amateur radio operator. Something about amateur radio's simplicity paired with its complexity sparked my curiosity. At first, I wondered "what is this amateur radio one speaks of." With the growth of the internet, this was not a difficult question to answer. 

Unlike many current operators, I am not a 50 year old guy. Like how many operators began, I'm 15. My young age is a driving force towards constantly learning, because there's a lot I don't know that I want to know. This very driving force allowed me to self study for a couple months to obtain my Basic qualification in Canada.

In 2024, I officially became certified as a true Canadian amateur radio operator with Basic with Honours. Currently, most of my time on frequencies are spent listening to conversations made in my local city: Regina, SK.
